# Shree Enterprises Sales Performance Dashboard

A data visualization project analyzing retail sales, product performance, and payment trends for educational supplies.

## 📊 Key Highlights
* **Total Revenue:** ₹637K (Across tracked period)
* **Top Category:** Footwear (driven by White PT Shoes at 0.33M)
* **Dominant Revenue Source:** Uniforms (1.91M) and Books (1.16M)
* **Seasonality:** Peak sales observed in **May**, aligning with the academic session start.
* **Payment Trends:** UPI is the leading payment mode at **45.4%**.

## 🚀 Insights
- **Product Mix:** While Uniforms bring in the most volume, Footwear is the highest-performing specific category.
- **Consumer Behavior:** A clear shift towards digital payments (UPI) over Cash (34.8%).
- **Trend Analysis:** Significant revenue dip post-July suggests a highly seasonal business model centered around the school reopening period.
